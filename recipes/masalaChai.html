Masala Chai

Description

Masala chai is a popular beverage throughout South Asia, originating in the early modern Indian subcontinent. Chai is made by brewing black tea in milk and water and then sweetening with sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cardamon pod
  • 1 cup of warm milk
  • 2 teaspoons of black tea leaves

Steps

  1. First start with boiling the 1 cup of water.
  2. after 1-2 minutes, add 2 cups of teaspoon of black tea leaves into the water and let it boil for some time
  3. Now add one cup of warm milk
  4. After adding warm milk add 1 teaspoon of sugar and let it boil on medium flame
  5. After few minutes, starin the chai into a cup and enjoy
