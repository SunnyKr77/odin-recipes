Palal Paneer

Description

A creamy spinach dish with cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese).

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust to spice preference)
  • 1 (10 oz) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
  • 1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • ¾ cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 cup paneer cubes, fried until golden brown (optional, can be skipped for a vegan dish)
  • Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan or pot. Add cumin seeds, let them sizzle for a few seconds. Add chopped onion and green chili (if using), cook until softened.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 30 seconds. Add coriander, turmeric, and red chili powder, stir and cook for a minute.
  3. Add thawed and squeezed spinach, cook until wilted.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and water. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add salt, garam masala, and lemon juice (if using). Blend the mixture using an immersion blender or transfer to a blender and blend until smooth.
  6. Return the blended mixture to the pan. Add fried paneer cubes (if using) and heat through for a couple of minutes.
  7. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with rice or roti.
